Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Decibel Pro is built by Cloud Motion Lab LLC. This policy explains what data the app handles and what it does not. The short version: the app analyzes sound on your device, never records or stores audio, and sends only anonymous usage data to keep the app running.

Information We Do Not Collect

Decibel Pro does not collect personal information. The app does not ask for your name, email, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or any account login. There is no user account.

Microphone Access

Decibel Pro requires microphone access to measure sound levels in real time. Audio is processed locally on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. The app reads sound pressure levels for display only. You can revoke microphone access at any time in iOS Settings › Privacy & Security › Microphone.

Anonymous Analytics

To understand how the app is used and to fix bugs, Decibel Pro sends anonymous, aggregated usage signals through TelemetryDeck. These signals contain no personally identifiable information. Examples include events like "onboarding completed" or "paywall shown." TelemetryDeck does not use cookies, advertising identifiers, or fingerprinting. See TelemetryDeck's privacy policy for details.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. To verify purchases and manage entitlements, Decibel Pro uses RevenueCat, which receives an anonymous Apple-provided transaction identifier. No personal information is shared. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for details.

Third Parties

The app uses the following services:

  • Apple — for App Store distribution and in-app purchases.
  • TelemetryDeck — anonymous, privacy-respecting usage analytics.
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase processing and entitlement management.

The app does not include third-party advertising SDKs and does not track users across apps or websites. The App Tracking Transparency prompt is not shown because no tracking takes place.

Data Retention

Anonymous analytics signals are retained by TelemetryDeck according to their retention policy. Purchase records are retained by Apple and RevenueCat as required for receipt validation and refund processing. The app itself stores only your settings and a counter of how many free measurements you have used, both kept on your device.

Children's Privacy

Decibel Pro is rated 4+. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. If you believe a child has somehow provided personal data, contact us and we will assist.

Your Rights

Because the app does not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You can stop all data flow at any time by deleting the app, which removes locally stored settings. Microphone permission can be revoked in iOS Settings.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will reflect the change. Material changes will be communicated within the app or in the App Store update notes.
